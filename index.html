φυλάσσω
phylássō
ANCIENT GREEK · VERB
"to guard, to watch,
to protect"

In ancient Greek, phylassō described the act of protecting something of great value — keeping it safe, unimpaired, and intact. Not simply watching over it, but actively ensuring nothing is lost.

That is the intention behind this app. Your household, your supplies, your plans — kept whole and ready. So that when the moment comes, nothing has slipped through the cracks.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No setup complexity. No manual configuration. Phylasso learns about you and starts working immediately.

Tell Phylasso who lives at home — adults, children, pets, any dietary or medical requirements. It tailors everything to your specific needs.

2

Scan barcodes or manually add your food, water, first aid supplies and tools. Phylasso tracks your readiness score and shows you exactly what's missing.
